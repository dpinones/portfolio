About

Building at the intersection
of gaming and blockchain

Software engineer with a background in Computer Science from UNGS and five years of experience in the tech industry — from consulting firms to Banco Santander, where I earned an early merit recognition.


In 2022, I transitioned to Web3 and haven't looked back. Through OnlyDust, I contributed to major Starknet projects like Beerus, Madara, and Kakarot. I participated in the Starknet Hacker Houses in Paris and Istanbul, and contributed to the Uniswap v3 migration to Starknet.


Today, as co-founder of Caravana Studio, I'm focused on bringing on-chain gaming to life with Jokers of Neon — built on Dojo Engine.
